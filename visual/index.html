Colors

Each color has a purpose and tells a different story. They will allow our users to interact more simply with our product. One of our main concern is to keep everything legible and accessible for everyone.

PRIMARY COLORS

Our primary colors are the most vibrant colors we use, they are the DNA of the iAdvize brand. These colors are mainly used for call-to-actions. Malachite is our main color for selected elements, and we use Azure for links and actions (with texts).
