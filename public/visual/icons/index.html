Icons

Either paired with language or alone, icons communicate a message, commands or actions. They should be clear and meaningful.

Grid & Size

All our icons are created on a 24px artboard. Even if we use Sketch to design our product, we remain attached to Adobe Illustrator to design icons.
Our icons are flat or they can contain lines, some of them even exist in both styles. Strokes are 2 pixels and have a rounded end.
We use two types of border radius, 2 pixels for small radius and 4 pixels for large radius. Icons should be made in geometric shapes.

How to use

Our icons are mostly used in tables, for channel representations or actions at the end of the line. We also use them for our main browsing in order to give more context to our users.
